Global Student Distribution

About This Visualization

This interactive map visualizes the global distribution of Berea College students as of Fall 2023. The data was provided by the Office of Institutional Research and Assessment, demonstrating our institution's commitment to fostering a diverse and inclusive learning environment.

The visualization highlights the international reach of Berea College's educational mission, showing how our community brings together students from across the globe.

Created by Nicholas Hamilton for the Diversity in Stem Committee
