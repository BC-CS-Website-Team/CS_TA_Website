Tech Ethics Committee

Tech Ethics is a monthly discussion forum focused on emerging technologies and their social impact. Through insightful discussions, we examine the ethical considerations inherent in technological advancements, addressing issues such as privacy, equity, sustainability, and autonomy.

Our Mission

The Tech Ethics Committee is dedicated to fostering meaningful discussions about the ethical implications of technology in our society. We aim to create a space where students can explore, debate, and understand the complex relationships between technology and human values.

Committee Goals

  • Organize monthly discussion sessions on emerging technology topics
  • Promote awareness of ethical considerations in technology development
  • Foster critical thinking about technology's impact on society
  • Build connections between technical skills and ethical responsibility
  • Engage with broader tech ethics community and initiatives

Recent Discussion Topics

  • Artificial Intelligence Ethics

    Exploring the ethical implications of AI development and deployment in society

  • Privacy in the Digital Age

    Understanding data privacy concerns and protecting user rights

  • Algorithmic Bias

    Examining fairness and bias in automated decision-making systems

  • Environmental Impact of Technology

    Discussing sustainable tech practices and environmental responsibility

Get Involved

Join our monthly discussions! We welcome all students interested in exploring the intersection of technology and ethics. No prior experience in ethics or technology required - just bring your curiosity and willingness to engage in meaningful dialogue.
